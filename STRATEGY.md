## 📣 Marketing Strategy

### SEO (Highest ROI, Long-term)
**Target keywords by volume:**
- "prompt engineering guide" — 18K/mo
- "AI coding tutorial" — 12K/mo
- "best AI tools 2025" — 45K/mo
- "ChatGPT prompts for [use case]" — 5K–30K/mo each
- "Midjourney tips" — 22K/mo

**Content strategy:**
- Publish 3 SEO articles/week
- Each article targets 1 primary + 3 secondary keywords
- Internal link all articles to course/prompt pages
- Build topical authority in: AI education, prompt engineering, AI tools

**Technical SEO checklist:**
- ✅ Next.js App Router (server-side rendering)
- ✅ Sitemap.xml auto-generated
- ✅ robots.txt configured
- ✅ Open Graph images for all pages
- ✅ Structured data (JSON-LD) on all pages
- ✅ Core Web Vitals optimized via Vercel
- ✅ Canonical URLs on all pages

### Social Media Strategy

**Twitter/X (@mgcreativelabs)**
- Post 2x/day: tips, prompts, tool reviews
- Engage with AI community (Andrej Karpathy, Sam Altman threads)
- Weekly "prompt of the day" — high shareability
- Target: 50K followers in 12 months

**YouTube (@mgcreativelabs)**
- 2 videos/week: tutorials, tool walkthroughs
- Thumbnail style: clean, high contrast, clear benefit
- Target keywords in titles for YouTube SEO
- Goal: 100K subscribers in 18 months

**LinkedIn**
- Target: freelancers, developers, entrepreneurs
- Post 1x/day: AI career advice, tool comparisons
- Long-form posts (>1000 words) get organic reach

**TikTok/Instagram Reels**
- 60-second AI tips and demos
- "AI did this in 30 seconds" format
- Viral potential for tool showcases

### Newsletter Strategy
- Weekly "AI Dispatch" newsletter
- Format: 1 tool highlight, 2 prompts, 1 tutorial, 1 news item
- Goal: 50K subscribers in 12 months
- Monetize with sponsor slots at $500–$2K/issue at 50K subs

### Community-Led Growth
- Prompt-sharing incentives (top contributors featured)
- "Build in public" weekly showcases
- Monthly AI project contests ($100–$500 prizes)
- Referral program: invite 3 friends → unlock 1 month Pro
